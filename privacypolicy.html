Privacy Policy


Last updated: April 21, 2024


Welcome to BookWorld! This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information to ensure your privacy and security when you use our website.

At BookWorld, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our services. By accessing or using BookWorld, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Interpretation and Definitions

For the purpose of clarity, certain terms used in this Privacy Policy are defined as follows:

Definitions:


  • Account: Your unique account created to access our services.

  • Affiliate: An entity related to BookWorld through ownership or control.

  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device by a website for various purposes.

  • Personal Data: Information that identifies or relates to you as an individual.

We collect various types of personal information to provide and improve our services, including:

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with trusted third parties for purposes such as data analysis, customer support, and marketing. We do not sell your information to third parties.

BookWorld uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic and enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

7. Children's Privacy

BookWorld does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

BookWorld may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at bookworld09@gmail.com.
